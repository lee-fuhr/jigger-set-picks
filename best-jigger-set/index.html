A good jigger is the unsung hero of consistent cocktails. Whether you are shaking up a classic daiquiri or a modern craft drink, precise measurements separate a balanced cocktail from a mess. The right jigger should be easy to read, comfortable to hold, and stable enough to pour without spilling. When choosing a jigger set, consider the material (stainless steel is the standard), the size of the measuring cups (most sets include 1 oz and 2 oz sides), and the clarity of the interior lines. If you frequently make drinks on the go, weight matters. If you are building a home bar from scratch, a complete set with a shaker and strainer might offer better value.
