How we choose our picks

We do not run timed trials or lab tests. Instead, we comb through hundreds of owner reviews, long-term feedback on bartending forums, and roundups from trusted cocktail publications to see which models genuinely last without fading, warping, or losing their accuracy. We also note what breaks: cheap jiggers often have painted-on markings that wear away, while good ones keep readable lines for years. Beyond durability, we weigh ergonomics and pour precision. A jigger that feels top-heavy or has a too-narrow lip can cause messy spills, so we look for designs that earn repeated praise for their balance and smooth pouring edge. Our final picks are the ones that the community consistently recommends as reliable, no-regret buys, not the ones with the slickest marketing.
